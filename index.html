Bio

I am a fifth-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Ohio State University. I am fortunate to work with my advisor, Prof. Alan Ritter. Before that, I obtained my MS degree in Electrical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University and my BS degree from the School of Electronic Science and Engineering at Nanjing University.

I mainly work on Natural Language Processing. I am interested in social media analysis and computational social science. Previously I have also worked on bandit algorithms and causal inference.
